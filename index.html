Prashad is an online and mobile prashad ordering system which we have developed for shopkepper owners and restaurant owners . Through prashad we are helping customers to get the prashad of ram mandir and other temple of Ayodhya. If you want to order prashad from our site just click on the order and choose your temple and get your order .

  • Prashad has grand plans to be India’s most loved hyperlocal player. It aims to be the most accessible platform on the network - through a variety of service offerings..
  • We also provide our delivery partners with transparent and flexible earning opportunities.
  • we provide restaurant partners with industry-specific marketing tools which enable them to engage and acquire customers to grow their business while also providing a reliable and efficient last mile delivery service.

We want our customers to focus on the more important things for themselves and not need to plan for the little things that life needs on an everyday basis. We are here to get your chores out of your way.
